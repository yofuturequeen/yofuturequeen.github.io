Overview and Usage

This game is called Strawberraroll. It features my favorite Sanrio character Cinnamaroll. Users play as Cinnamaroll to collect strawberries and flours to bake a cake. Don't collect the poison though! Or your game will be over. You can play my game here.

Issue Deep-Dive

I ran into a lot of problems with this game. The most prominent issue having to do with removing objects once they've touched/overlapped. I think this issue had to do with logic as every time Cinnamaroll would overlap a strawberry, nothing would happen. I looked at tons of examples online and actually copied over the syntax from the reference to remove the strawberry. For the life of me, I could not figure out why it wouldn't work. I tried rewriting the code to accomplish this fundamental task at least 10 times. I would get errors like remove not being a function or the overlap simply not working which was really frusturating. Finally though, one of my methods worked and the strawberries would remove. Even now, I don't understand what I was doing wrong but at least it works!

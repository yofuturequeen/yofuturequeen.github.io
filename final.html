Final Project Writeup

Overview and Usage

This game is called Strawberraroll and is the final version of my game. Play as Sanrio's Cinnamaroll and collect strawberries and flour to create a cake! Make sure to avoid the poison or your cake will be ruined. Strawberraroll gets increasingly harder as you continue to play and players can keep track of their high scores with a leaderboard. Play a game of Strawberraroll here!

Issue Deep-Dive

The biggest problem I ran into for finishing up this game was adding Firebase. I would say this was mainly logic error as I felt completely unfamiliar with using it and adding to my existing code. With Firebase, there are a lot of components that link together and I think it was most difficult for me to figure out what should in which file. What I mean by this is differentiating what needs to go in my html file vs js file and then learning how they should commmunicate with one another.
